Bob's chocolate chip cookies

  225g butter, softened
  200g brown sugar
  100g white sugar
  2 eggs
   2 tsp vanilla
   3 cups flour
   1 tsp baking soda
   1 tsp salt
   2 cups chocolate chips

Beat butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Whisk dry
ingredients separately, then combine. Fold in chips.
Drop tablespoons onto sheet, 350F for 10 min.
